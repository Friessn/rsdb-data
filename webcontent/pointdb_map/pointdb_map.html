Explorer of LiDAR point cloud

overview map

At the lower section a overview map is shown. There you can choose a layer and a map-scale.

Left-mouse-click on the map to get a detail view of that position.

Shift-key + left-mouse-click to get a messagebox with position (e.g. to copy postion to clipboard).

detail image

At the upper section a detail view is shown. There you can choose image scale, type of visualisation and option to fill empty pixels.

Left-mouse-click on the image to move detail view to center of this position.

Shift-key + left-mouse-click to get a messagebox with position (e.g. to copy postion to clipboard).

Ctrl-key + left-mouse-click to get image in full page dimensions.
